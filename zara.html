ZARA

Zara is one of the largest international fashion companies. It belongs to Inditex, one of the world's largest distribution groups. The customer is at the heart of our unique business model, which includes design, production, distribution and sales through our extensive retail network.

The clothing retailer has more nearly 3,000 stores, including its kids and home stores. It has stores in 96 countries and is the flagship brand of the Inditex Group. Zara is renowned for its ability to develop a new product and get it to stores within two weeks, while other retailers take six months. Spain is the biggest market with 547 stores (including Zara Kids and Zara Home), followed by China (229 stores), France (145), Russia (144) and Italy (134). .
