THE PENTHOUSE

MAIN ACTORS:Lee Ji-ah,Kim So-yeon,Eugene,Um Ki-joon,Yoon Jong-hoon,Shin Eun-kyung,Bong Tae-kyu,Park Eun-seok,Yoon Joo-hee

>AGE RESTRICTION:16 and older as it contains violence

SYNOPSIS OF THE MOVIE:Penthouse tells the story of wealthy families living in Hera Palace and their children at Cheong-ah Arts School. Shim Su-ryeon (Lee Ji-ah) is an elegant, wealthy woman who has a tragic past. Her husband is Joo Dan-tae (Uhm Ki-joon), a successful businessman. She later learns that he is hiding a secret from her.
